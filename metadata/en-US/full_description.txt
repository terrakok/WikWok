WikWok transforms your Wikipedia reading experience into an engaging, scroll-based article feed. Learn something new with every swipe!

Key Features
- Beautiful Interface: Clean, modern design focused on readability
- Intuitive Scrolling Feed: Discover Wikipedia articles in an engaging format
- Cross-Platform: Available on multiple platforms including Android, iOS, desktop and web
- Completely Free: No subscriptions, no ads, just knowledge
- Open Source: Community-driven development welcomes contributions

Enjoy a fresh way to discover knowledge! Simply scroll through beautifully formatted Wikipedia articles and expand your horizons one swipe at a time.
WikWok is and will always remain completely free with no advertisements.
If you enjoy the app, consider supporting the developer with a coffee through the link in the app!
